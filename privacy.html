Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Joshua Hart ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Next Batter Up mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide: Player names, team names, and lineup configurations that you enter into the App. This data is stored locally on your device.

Apple Music data: The App accesses your Apple Music library to play walk-up songs. We do not store, transmit, or collect your Apple Music listening history or library contents.

Audio recordings: If you choose to record custom voice announcements, these recordings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Automatically collected information: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as app launches and feature usage) through Apple's built-in App Analytics. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above solely to:

  • Provide and improve the App's functionality
  • Enable you to create and manage team lineups and walk-up music
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance

3. Data Storage & Security

All player data, team data, lineups, and audio recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. If you use the App's sharing features, data is transmitted directly between devices using Apple's sharing frameworks.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Apple Music: Used to play walk-up songs. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Analytics: Provides anonymous, aggregated usage statistics. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and may be used to manage lineups for youth sports teams. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Player names entered into the App are stored only on the device and are not transmitted to us. Parents or guardians who believe their child's information has been collected should contact us so we can address the concern.

6. Your Choices

You can delete all App data at any time by deleting the App from your device. You can also manage individual teams and players within the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
